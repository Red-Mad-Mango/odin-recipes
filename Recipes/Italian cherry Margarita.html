Italian cherry Margarita

How you can make it

In this Italian cherry margarita, amaretto and fresh cherries join up with lime and tequila for a refreshing cocktail.

Ingredients

  • fresh cherries, pitted, or more to taste, plus more for garnish
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces reposado tequila
  • 1 fluid ounce amaretto liqueur

Steps

  1. Place cherries and lime juice in a shaker and muddle to loosen the cherry juices.

  2. Fill shaker with ice, add tequila and amaretto, and shake until well blended and slightly frothy. Strain over ice and garnish with a cherry.
